Solving Goa’s Waste Challenges

Together, we can reduce pollution and protect Goa’s beauty.

Start Learning

Why It Matters

Goa is struggling with waste due to rapid tourism and urban growth. Improper disposal harms health, marine life, and the beauty of our land.

What You’ll Learn

  • How to properly segregate waste at home
  • Where to find collection centers
  • How to participate in clean-up initiatives

Major Waste Issues in Goa

  • 🚯 Garbage dumping on beaches and streets
  • 🚫 Lack of public awareness about waste types
  • ♻️ Improper waste segregation at the household level
  • 🧃 Use of single-use plastics and packaging
  • 📉 Inefficient recycling systems

Our Goal

This website promotes responsible waste behavior and connects people to resources, success stories, and tools to reduce environmental damage.
